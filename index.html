Featured Projects

Three featured projects. Real outcomes.

Brand Strategy & Operational Excellence

Global Brand Architecture & Workflow Automation — iLiveIt

Situation
The organisation operated across two distinct global markets (South Africa and Australia) without a unified visual strategy or an efficient production pipeline. Manual design workflows were creating significant bottlenecks, slowing down the delivery of critical assets for high-stakes retail and banking clients.
Task
My brief was to architect a cohesive international brand identity while simultaneously restructuring the creative department's operations to improve speed, scalability, and output quality across both continents.
Action
I directed the end-to-end production of digital and print collateral and developed bespoke, personalised HTML email marketing systems for major sectors. To solve the operational delays, I identified inefficiencies and introduced automation tools and restructured workflows. I utilised the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator) and After Effects to maintain premium visual standards while managing and mentoring a high-performance remote team.
Result
The implementation of automated workflows led to a dramatic reduction in project turnaround times and operational overhead. I successfully maintained brand integrity across international borders, reduced staff turnover through targeted mentorship, and delivered high-conversion digital campaigns that strengthened the brand's position in the global market.
Operational Leadership & Business Transformation

Strategic Operations & Marketing Management — INRATIME / Castle Da Angelo & Loui-Giano Wedding Venues

Situation
The business required a high-level operational lead to manage luxury wedding and event venues (Castle Da Angelo and Loui-Giano) during a period of transition. The challenge was to maintain premium service standards while managing multi-million rand budgets and navigating complex vendor relations in a high-pressure environment.
Task
My goal was to oversee daily operations and resource allocation while revitalising the brand's market presence through strategic marketing to ensure high guest satisfaction and consistent repeat bookings.
Action
I directed all operational workflows and financial management, including the negotiation of vendor contracts to maximise ROI. I implemented digital coordination tools to streamline booking processes and introduced eco-friendly waste reduction initiatives to improve sustainability. Additionally, I spearheaded digital marketing strategies and public relations efforts to align the brand's visual identity with its premium physical experience.
Result
The operational overhaul resulted in a highly efficient business model with improved guest feedback and strong financial performance. My strategic marketing initiatives directly contributed to a robust booking pipeline, ensuring the long-term sustainability and prestige of the venues within the luxury market.
Bespoke Craftsmanship & Sensory Experience

Floral Artistry & Aesthetic Direction — INRATIME Events

Situation
For high-end luxury weddings and corporate events, the aesthetic experience depends heavily on the physical details. The venues required a lead who could not only manage the business but also step in to ensure the floral arrangements and decor met an exacting, premium standard that aligned with the client's bespoke vision.
Task
My goal was to bridge the gap between the digital mood boards and the physical reality of the event. I took hands-on responsibility for the design and assembly of intricate bridal bouquets and floral installations, ensuring they served as the centrepiece of the event's visual narrative.
Action
I personally curated, sourced, and hand-crafted floral arrangements, applying principles of colour theory, texture, and composition usually reserved for high-end graphic design. I managed the delicate balance of perishables and logistics, ensuring every bouquet was in an impeccable state for the "hero moments" of the ceremony. This required physical stamina, creative problem-solving under tight deadlines, and a deep understanding of the client's emotional requirements.
Result
These hand-crafted details significantly elevated the perceived value of the venue's service, resulting in high guest satisfaction and "Instagram-worthy" moments that fuelled the brand's social proof. It proved my ability as a leader to "get my hands dirty" to ensure excellence, demonstrating that my creative eye translates perfectly from the screen to the physical world.
