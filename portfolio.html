Creative Mentorship · Community Engagement

Traditional Arts & Crafts Mentorship Case Study

Situation
High school youth in the Hartbeespoort community required expert guidance to master a wide array of traditional crafts to achieve national accreditation and earn their proficiency badges.
Task
Facilitate an immersive, hands-on mentorship programme for the local Kommando GGN, focusing on technical mastery across diverse physical mediums.
Action
Led intensive workshops in candle and soap making, fabric painting, and structural work with cement, wire, and paper. I simplified complex artisanal techniques—from wool-work to cable manipulation—into actionable milestones for the youth groups.
Result
Empowered the next generation of makers with practical, lifelong creative skills and successfully guided all participants through the completion of their national badge requirements.
